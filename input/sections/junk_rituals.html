This project started from the discover in a closing down sale of a circular sawblade, a device with a particular sound reminescent of ritual instruments like temple bells and singing bowls, while still retaining some of the industrial clang. From this the idea of a pseudo-ritual instrument made from the sinergy of the sawblade sound and projected everchanging visuals, a union of the concrete sound of the material and the abstract nature of light and code.

Realized with a piezo sensor, an Arduino microcontroller, Raspberry Pi, a pico projector and a glockenspiel mallet for playing the sawblade. Coded with Arduino IDE and openFrameworks.

Exhibited at Flussi Festival 2014.
